HOW TO REPLACE A SAFARICOM SIMCARD
1.locate a service provider shop
2.Buy a new replacement sim
3.Take a new safaricom cards and and insert it into your device 
4.Contact safaricom customer care
5.Confirm ownership of the line to be replaced 
6.Dial #IMS number
7.Enter your mobile number 
8.Enter original pin
9.Activate your mpesa on new line
10.If the detail given is correct then your sim is replaced 
11.If not the safaricon provider sends you a message
